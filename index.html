Premium Quality, Every Time

Premium quality fabric with high-quality printing. DTF (Direct to Film) transfers are heat-bonded onto the fabric — colours stay vivid and crack-free wash after wash. DTG (Direct to Garment) is printed directly into the fabric fibres, giving a softer feel with a beautiful vintage look that develops over time.
