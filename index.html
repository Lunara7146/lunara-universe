🔄 Returns Guide

Follow these steps to request a return for manufacturer defects:

📸 Step 1: Take Photos

Capture clear images of the defect and the packaging as it arrived. Include any keyword codes or links printed on the packaging.

📝 Step 2: Submit Evidence

Email your photos, order details, and any packaging codes/links to lunarasuniverse@gmail.com within 7 days of delivery. Requests submitted after 7 days will not be accepted.

📦 Step 3: Return Item

Once approved, ship the product back in its original condition and packaging. Items damaged after delivery will not qualify.

💳 Step 4: Receive Credits

After verification, Lunara Credits will be added to your account for future purchases. No cash refunds are provided.

📑 Terms & Conditions

Strict return policy: only manufacturer defects qualify for Lunara Credits. Customers must provide photo and packaging evidence. No refunds for preference-based returns.
